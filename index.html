Hello! I work on Computer Vision and Computational Fabrication Techqiues. As a Physics and Computer Science student, I've always been fascinated by the intersections between the physical universe and computational systems. My work involves leveraging computational models and algorithms to solve complex problems in Physics, and using the principles of Physics to improve computing technology.

In Physics, my research focuses on Quantum Mechanics, especially Quantum Computing. I am currently exploring the development of quantum algorithms to significantly speed up computations compared to classical algorithms. My ultimate goal is to contribute to the evolution of a new generation of superfast quantum computers.
